## 1. About Me

I am a pharmaceutical chemist specializing in cheminformatics and computer-aided drug design, with a PhD in Chemical Sciences from UNAM and international research experience in Italy and Chile. My work focuses on virtual library design, machine learning-based virtual screening, and ADME-Tox property evaluation for drug discovery, currently as a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute for Obesity Research, Tecnológico de Monterrey. With over 20 publications in indexed journals, I combine technical research with a strong commitment to mentoring and scientific communication.

## 2. Projects

### Database Analysis and Characterization

- **NuBBE Database** — Chemoinformatic characterization of the chemical space and diversity of this natural products database from Brazil (*J. Chem. Inf. Model.*, 2019).
- **BIOFACQUIM** — Development of a Mexican compound database of natural products, used as a resource for virtual screening across Latin American research groups (*Biomolecules*, 2019).
- **Lactam Chemical Space** — Cheminformatic exploration of the chemical space and bioactivity profile of lactams (*RSC Adv.*, 2019), including a scaffold analysis of bicyclic acetals and their relevance in diversity-oriented synthesis (*Org. Biomol. Chem.*, 2019).
- Development of cheminformatics workflows to characterize natural product databases in support of virtual screening efforts across Latin American research groups (Master's research project).

### Design of Virtual Libraries and Drug Optimization

- **Antidiabetic Focused Library** — PhD project applying literature-derived chemical transformation rules to design a multi-target focused virtual library for antidiabetic targets, predicting compound activity against emerging biological targets in Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus (*Front. Pharmacol.*, 2023).
- **Diversity-Oriented Synthesis of Lactams** — Computational design of a lactam library through diversity-oriented synthesis strategies, developed during a research stay at the University of Florence, Italy, including the computational encoding of chemical reactions to automate synthesis protocols (*Bioorg. Med. Chem.*, 2020).
- **De Novo Design of DNMT1 Inhibitors** — Comparative analysis of ligand- and structure-based de novo design approaches for DNA methyltransferase 1 inhibitors (*Biomolecules*, 2024), and consensus docking strategies inspired by de novo design (*Front. Drug Discov.*, 2023).
- **HIV-1 Protease Inhibitors** — Contribution to the de novo design of HIV-1 protease inhibitors based on natural products (*Biomolecules*, 2021).

### Virtual Screening and Activity Prediction

- **AlzyFinder Platform** — Co-development of a machine-learning-driven platform for ligand-based virtual screening and network pharmacology, applied to the discovery of bioactive compounds against Alzheimer's disease-related targets (*J. Chem. Inf. Model.*, 2024) — developed during a research stay at the University of Concepción, Chile; awarded Best Poster Presentation at the Third Meeting of the Chilean Bioinformatics Society (2024).
- **Antiviral Compound Prediction** —  Development of predictive models for identifying compounds with antiviral activity (2025).

### ADMET

- **ADME-Tox Web Server Development** — Current postdoctoral work at the Institute for Obesity Research, Tecnológico de Monterrey, developing web servers for the evaluation of ADME-Tox (absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion, and toxicity) properties of bioactive compounds.
